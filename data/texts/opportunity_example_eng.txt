Actions covered by this notice  
This notice covers actions promoted by companies, as provided for in Article 50 of Ordinance No. 
559/2023 of July 25, relating to: 
a) Productive investments as well as investments that promote decarbonization, the use of renewable energy 
and energy efficiency, the circular economy, digitization, and internationalization,  
b) Investments that promote entrepreneurship by supporting the creation and development of start-ups
and spin-offs; 
c) Investments in the certification and promotion of sustainable fisheries and aquaculture products, including 
the processes leading to the registration of trademarks or patents;  
d) Investments that reduce the impact of the activity on the environment;  
e) Development of marketing and internationalization strategies, including promotional 
or prospecting and product development actions that are not part of actions organized by 
producers' associations and organizations;  The European Funds closest to you.  
           
 
          2/23 
f) Marketing innovation , involving the implementation of a new marketing method  with significant changes 
in product design or packaging, or in its promotion and distribution;  
g) Initiatives that promote consumption diversification through the processing of fishery products 
relating to more abundant species with lower commercial value;  
h) Promotion of short distribution and marketing circuits.  
Projects in co-promotion innovation may also be supported, provided they are led by a company, within the 
meaning of Article 23(2)(c) of DL 20-A/2023, namely:  
i) projects developed between two or more independent entities that cooperate strategically 
and effectively in the medium and long term, sharing infrastructure, skills, and resources, 
including human resources, according to their specific contributions to the project's objectives, with a view to 
developing the actions that make up the common activity plan and achieving the objectives 
and results;  
ii) in which the company assumes the role of lead entity, being responsible for ensuring the overall coordination 
of the project and the dialogue with the various co-promoters and between them and the managing authority, with 
regard to the technical, administrative, and financial management of the project;  
iii) in which a written agreement must be established, signed by all entities participating in the 
project, which specifies the scope of cooperation between the entities involved, the identification of the 
lead entity, the joint responsibility between the parties, the individual contribution of each entity to the 
implementation of the project, as well as the terms of sharing costs, risks, and results;  
iv) in which all entities participating in the project are considered beneficiaries and must therefore 
comply with all eligibility requirements, obligations, and impediments of beneficiaries;  
and 
v) payments are made to all co-promoters, who are individually responsible 
for the repayment of the support they have received and jointly responsible for the pursuit of the 
project's objectives, as well as for the achievement of the results .

Eligible actions  
The actions provided for in Article 50 of Ordinance No. 559/2023, of July 25, are eligible for this notice: 
a) Productive investments as well as investments that promote decarbonization, the use of renewable energies 
and energy efficiency, the circular economy, digitization, and internationalization, including 
those that:  
i) Improve environmental and climate performance;  
ii) Strengthen food security;  
iii) Promote the introduction of new species on the market, namely through the valorization of 
fish with lower commercial value;  
iv) Promote the processing of by-products resulting from the main processing activities;  
v) Promote the valorization of aquaculture products;  
vi) Are innovative, promoted by companies or in co-promotion with universities or research centers,
 provided they are led by the company;  
vii) Promote the use of renewable energy and improved energy performance, optimization of the use 
of water resources;  
viii) Promote the use of bio-based, biodegradable, or recyclable packaging, or other 
initiatives that reduce the use of paper or plastic;  
ix) Contribute to the reduction of food waste through the introduction of innovative solutions 
in the processing and marketing of fish;  
b) Promotion of entrepreneurship through support for the creation and development of start-ups and spin-offs;  
c) Investments in the certification and promotion of sustainable fisheries and aquaculture products, including 
the processes leading to the registration of trademarks or patents;  
d) Investments that reduce the impact of the activity on the environment;  
e) Development of marketing and internationalization strategies, including promotional 
or prospecting and product development actions that are not part of actions organized by 
producers' associations and organizations;  
f) Marketing innovation, involving the implementation of a new marketing method with significant changes 
in product design or packaging, or in its promotion and distribution;  
g) Initiatives that promote consumption diversification through the processing of fishery products 
relating to more abundant species with lower commercial value;  
h) Promotion of short distribution and marketing channels .

Specific conditions or technical standards to be observed by beneficiaries or operations  
With regard to beneficiaries, without prejudice to the eligibility criteria set out in Article 14 of Decree-Law
No. 20-A/2023, of March 22, which establishes the general regime for the application of European funds in Portugal 
2030 and the general criteria defined in Article 5 of Ordinance No. 559/2023, of July 25 , the beneficiaries of 
this notice must also comply with the requirements set out in Article 53 of the same Ordinance . 
a) Hold SME status, based on certification from IAPMEI;  
b) Hold an installation permit, in the case of the construction of new establishments;  
c) Hold an operating license and veterinary control number, in the case of the modernization of 
existing establishments; 
d) Hold authorization for changes to establishments that require licensing, where applicable;  
e) Present a balanced economic and financial situation.  
For this purpose, a balanced economic and financial situation is considered to exist when the 
pre-project financial autonomy is equal to or greater than 15%, based on the last financial year closed on the date of 
submission of the application.  The European Funds closest to you.
